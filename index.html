Praveen Choppala

Welcome to my homepage. My research interests intersect the areas of statistical signal processing, machine learning and Bayesian filtering. I work in several application domains including target tracking, audio and image denoising and acoustic souce localisation. I obtained my PhD from the Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand. Thereafter I held two postdoctoral positions, the first at the Robinson Reseach Institute, Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand, and the second at the University of New South Wales, Australia.
