Praveen Choppala

Welcome to my homepage. My research interests intersect the areas of statistical signal processing, machine learning and Bayesian filtering. I work in several application domains including target tracking, audio and image denoising, acoustic souce localisation and econometrics. I obtained my PhD from the Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand. Thereafter I held two postdoctoral positions, the first at the Robinson Reseach Institute, Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand, and the second at the (QS 20th ranked) University of New South Wales, Sydney Australia. I am privileged to have been supervised by Professors Paul Teal and Marcus Frean at the Victoria University of Wellington and Professors Robert Kohn and Sally Cripps at the University of New South Wales.

I am currently with the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Andhra University, India.
